Vision and Mission of Utkal Jagruti Seva Sangha

UtkalJagruti Seva Sangh is a socio-cultural registered organization.
The aim and objectives of the organisaton are as follows:-
1) To bring all Oriya organization in Mumbai to one platform forthe betterment of society.
2) To help financially to poor and needy students.
3) To arrange blood donation camp and medical camps.
4) To unit people on the basis of our great culture.
5) To revive our great ancient culture and heritage.
6) It's motto - Service to mankind is service to God (Manava seva hi madhaba seva).
7) To create awareness for national integration. To keep vigilon anti national activities.
8) To work selflessly in the other organization.
9) To provide relief at the time of natural calamities.
